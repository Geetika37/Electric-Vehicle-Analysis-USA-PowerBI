**Electric Vehicle Analysis Dashboard (USA)**

**Project Overview**
This project presents an Electric Vehicle (EV) Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI.
The dashboard analyzes the growth and distribution of electric vehicles across the United States, focusing on vehicle types, manufacturers, geographic distribution, and market trends.

The objective of this project is to identify key trends in EV adoption, understand which companies dominate the market, and analyze the growth of electric vehicles over the years.

 **Key Insights**
From the dashboard analysis, the following insights were discovered:

- Washington State has the highest electric vehicle distribution, showing strong EV adoption in that region.
- Tesla dominates the EV market in the United States, accounting for 46% of all EV vehicles in the dataset.
- Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV) represent about 78% of total EVs, indicating that fully electric vehicles are more widely adopted than plug-in hybrid vehicles.
- EV production and adoption have increased steadily every year, showing strong growth in the electric vehicle market.
- CAFV Eligibility Analysis shows that most vehicles qualify for Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle benefits, indicating strong support for environmentally friendly transportation policies.
- Tesla Model Y and Tesla Model 3 are the most popular EV models, making them the top contributors to EV adoption in the dataset.
